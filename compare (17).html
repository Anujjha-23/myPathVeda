Key Takeaways

How to Choose Your Program?

💸

Budget-Conscious

IGNOU (₹63K) and LPU Online (₹1.2L) offer the best value. Government jobs also accept IGNOU degrees.

🏆

Brand Value

NMIMS and Symbiosis carry the strongest brand value for corporate roles, especially in finance and consulting.

📊

Analytics / Tech MBA

Chandigarh University and Amity Online offer strong business analytics specializations with tech upskilling.

🌐

International Recognition

Manipal and NMIMS degrees are accepted across GCC countries, USA, and UK for further studies or work.
